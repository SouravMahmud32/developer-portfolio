Jhonny Depps Bio:

Johnny Depp is an American actor and musician. He first broke into stardom in 1987 when he starred in the TV series 21 Jump Street. Depp perhaps best known for portraying Jack Sparrow in the Pirates of the Caribbean films -- which earned him the first of his three Academy Award nominations -- and his frequent collaborations with director Tim Burton. He is the father of model and actress Lily-Rose Depp.
